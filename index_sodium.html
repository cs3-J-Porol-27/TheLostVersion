The Lost Version

by: Joriz Zianne D. Porol

As I drown myself in the plethora of emotions and oppositions

I recall the permanent sepia hue that obscured my vision.

The version of me that I once called my home,

Gone, as I wait for the waves to swallow me whole.

All the memories consume me,

And the thoughts disipate from my reach.

I find myself in a place where apathy was no longer,

And I knew all I had to do was breathe.
